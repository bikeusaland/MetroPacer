Privacy Policy

MetroPacer · Last updated 16 July 2026

MetroPacer does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data we collect

None. MetroPacer has no user accounts, no sign-in, and no analytics or tracking. We do not collect your name, email, location, contacts, health data, device identifiers, or usage data.

Data stored on your device

Your app settings — such as cadence, click sound, volume, accent, subdivision, and flash/vibrate preferences — are saved locally on your device so they persist between launches. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. Deleting the app removes it.

Audio

MetroPacer plays a metronome sound over any audio you are already playing (such as music or a podcast). It does not access your microphone, and it does not read, record, or transmit any audio.

Third parties

MetroPacer contains no third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs. No data is sent to us or to anyone else.

Children

MetroPacer is safe for all ages and does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
